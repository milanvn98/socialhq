Compatible NFC Devices

The iPhone 7 onwards all have NFC functionality providing IOS 14 has been installed. The latest generations of iPhones can read NFC tags without an additional app, straight out of the box!

The iPhone 7, 8 and X have a built in NFC reader which can be actived in the control panel (swiping up on iPhone 7 & 8, swiping down on iPhone X.)
