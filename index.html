Reinventing the way we connect.

Your Social Tag is powered by NFC technology for a contactless way to network with the community around you. For all the social butterflies out there, our tag allows you to share all your social platforms at once.

This smart tag sticks to your mobile device making it easy to stay connected to the people you meet. No third party apps required! Simply tap your NFC enabled device* to the tag and share your Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter, TikTok and more!

How it Works

1. Order your Social Tag

Simply order your social tag and we will encode your tag with a personal link to your SocialHQ. Using NFC technology, you are able to link your entire digital presence to a simple smart device.

2. Create your virtual identity

Create your profile and link your social media accounts to your SocialHQ. Personalise your profile to include social networks like Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook and so much more!

*If you there is a social network of your preference not listed on your profile, please contact customer support and we will customise your page.

3. Just stick it!

Once your personal social tag arrives, simply peel and stick the tag to the back of your phone as a fashionable accessory. Or stick the tag to your wallet, watch or forehead! The possibilities are limitless!

*Tags perform best when placed on the outide of phone cases.

4. Connect & Share

You are now ready to share your virtual identity with the world around you. You can copy and paste your social link into any bio to allow followers access to your SocialHQ. Or simply tap your tag to the back of your new connections phone and watch your socials appear!
